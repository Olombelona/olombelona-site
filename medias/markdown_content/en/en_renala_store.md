**Renala** turns your disk usage into a visual map so you can spot large files instantly.

### Built for macOS clarity

- **Interactive treemap** to see what takes space at a glance
- **Fast drill-down navigation** from volume to folder to file
- **Sidebar search and filtering** to focus on what matters
- **Privacy-first design**: scans run locally on your Mac
