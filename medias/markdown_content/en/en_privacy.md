## Data We Collect

We do not collect, store or share any personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily provide it (for example by contacting us via email).

Our apps may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (crash reports, performance metrics) solely through Apple's built-in analytics framework, subject to your device's privacy settings.

## Use of Data

Any data collected is used exclusively to:

- Respond to support requests

## Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above.

## Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate third-party advertising, tracking or analytics SDKs beyond Apple's native frameworks.

## Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
